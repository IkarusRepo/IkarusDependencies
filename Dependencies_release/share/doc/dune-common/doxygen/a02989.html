template<class Head, class Tail>
struct Dune::JoinTuples< Head, Tail >

Join two std::tuple's.

For Head=std::tuple<T0,...,TN> and Tail=std::tuple<S0,...,SM> the exported result is std::tuple<T0,..,TN,S0,...,SM>.
